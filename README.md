## Lumi App 

## Features

- **Personal AI Assistant**  
  Students get their own AI assistant that helps create **error-free projects, apps, programs, notes, ML projects, PPTs, and games** in **any programming or scripting language**.  

- **Student Library System**  
  All student-generated content – projects, notes, PPTs, apps, and games – is automatically saved in a **personal library** for easy access, management, and reuse.

- **AI Premier Label & Monetization Guidance**  
  - Any content that is **sellable** gets an **AI Premier label**.  
  - AI provides **step-by-step guidance** on **where and how to sell** content, including tutorial links and platform titles.  

- **Task Checklist & Earnings Guidance System**  
  - Students receive **detailed step-by-step guidance** to create earning accounts (example: “Payooner”), including NIC verification, SIM registration, and bank account setup.  
  - **Progress bar tracking** shows step completion (Step 1 Complete, Step 2 Complete, etc.).  
  - Once setup is done, the student receives a **“Successfully Completed” status** and a **motivational badge** to encourage completion.  
  - Tutorials and platform guidance ensure students can start earning safely and efficiently.  

- **Interactive Tutorials**  
  Step-by-step instructions for using the app, AI assistant, and monetization tools effectively.  

- **Multi-Theme Support** *(Coming Soon)*  
  UI customization with cute, aesthetic, and study-themed designs will be available in future updates once students start earning through the app.

- **Fake Dataset Integration**  
  For testing and learning purposes, the app can work with **fake datasets (`false.js`)** before connecting to real data.
